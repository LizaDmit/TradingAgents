- The stock moved from the low/mid-90s in early April to the mid-130s by late May.

### Why HOLD rather than BUY

A new long entry here has some positives:
- price is above key moving averages
- MACD is supportive
- RSI is strong but not extreme

But there are also reasons to be patient:
- the move has already been substantial
- RSI has recently been near overbought
- short-term momentum is positive but not as steep as before
- ATR implies a normal pullback could easily shake out weak entries

So the best stance is **HOLD** for traders already in position, or **wait for a better pullback / confirmation** before initiating fresh exposure.
